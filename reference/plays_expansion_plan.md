## Structural Gaps to Fill

Four categories of plays currently missing from the library:

1. **Post-climax / reinforcement-phase plays** — plays that deploy in days 1–30 after the denouement. Currently the 3R model has Revelation and Replication but the Reinforcement touchpoints are thin.

2. **Resistance handling plays** — participant disengages, expresses skepticism, tests the fiction, or tries to break the fourth wall. Current library has no plays for this.

3. **Failure recovery plays** — a prior play misfired: wrong delivery, participant didn't notice, artifact was too obvious. Plays that salvage the arc.

4. **Multi-participant plays** — two participants whose arcs intersect, or a secondary character who is also a participant. Emergent play space.
