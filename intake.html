E — Calibration & Limits
E1What do you want them to be able to do, feel, or understand after this experience that they can't — or don't — right now?
Most important question on this form. Be as specific as you can — "feel better" is not enough.
E2Is there anything that would be particularly wrong for them? Content, contexts, types of experience to categorically avoid.
E3What's happening in their life right now that we should know about?
E4Setting aside the transformation you described above — what shape of experience would feel exactly right for them? Not the outcome, but the form: the setting, the pace, the degree of involvement.

F1Describe a time they were genuinely surprised by something in their own life — not just a nice surprise, but something that shifted their perspective or expectation. What happened, and how did they respond?
