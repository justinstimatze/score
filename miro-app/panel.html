How to use this tool

  1. Browse the Plays tab to find plays. Click a play to expand its metadata. Drag or click the ⠿ handle to add it as a card on the board.
  2. Arrange cards left → right in arc order.
  3. Select cards (or leave none selected to use all) and click Check Arc to validate the sequence.
  4. In the Analyze tab, expand the Profile section to set up a participant profile, then click Analyze for Profile to score each play. Click any play name in the results to jump back to its library entry.

Card titles

Start the title with the play name — capitalisation and spaces are fine. Anything after a dash, bracket, or other separator is ignored: Certified Mail — day 3, certified_mail (act 1).

Optional card metadata

Add any of these to the card's description field, space-separated:

Arc types

Investigation — participant discovers a hidden truth
Initiation — threshold crossing; must define an early exit
Persecution — participant is pursued or tested
Reckoning — confrontation with something from the past
Conspiracy — uncovering a hidden structure
Test — skill or courage ordeal
